Endangered Birds and Animals of Sikkim

The Birds and Animals of almost all regions of the world are Endangered. The Indian state Sikkim is well known for its natural beauty but it is loosing that too because of human greeds. There are many species of birds and animals which are only found in Sikkim and nearby regions which are a symbol of proud for Sikkim. But shamefully, they are now also Endangered. One such example of endangered animal is Red Panda which is also the State Animal of Sikkim. But now along with the honor of being the state animal its name is in the list of Most Endangered animals of sikkim.
